Accelerating Digital Transformation from Endurance to Excellence

TechRun is a unique forum that draws on this cultural metaphor showcasing Kenya’s strengths in both athletics and technology. The event positions innovation and entrepreneurship as a “marathon, not a sprint,” emphasizing the long-term vision required to drive impactful innovation and sustainable economic growth.

Opening & Policy Dialogues

Day One features innovation and exhibitor showcases, opening remarks from Eldohub & partners, an overview and official opening of TechRun 2025, startup clinics, the AI demo zone, an Eldohub tour with beneficiary success stories, the Bold Stage on AI policy and ethics, and breakout roundtables on AI in sports, startup growth, and future skills.

Venue: TAC Center(Central Primary School Grounds) & Eldohub
Register
  • 29 November 2025
  • Day Two

Innovation & Collaboration

Day Two features the Bold Stage with raw debates on digital inclusion and AI adoption, keynotes and fireside chats on sports, the Eldohub Tech Academy graduation, an IoT acceleration showcase, Pitch Perfect presentations by kids and teens, Hackathon Demo Day with AI-in-sport solutions.

Venue: TAC Center(Central Primary School Grounds) & Eldohub
